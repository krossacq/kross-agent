# SOP 48: Bank Product And Fee Setup

Version: 0.1
Owner: Tax Mogul Bureau
Applies to: Kross Agent for Firms
Phase: 7 - TaxSlayer Data Entry

## Purpose

Define Kross boundaries around bank product and fee setup in TaxSlayer.

## Core Rule

Bank product and fee setup is high-risk. Kross may stage or review information only when the firm has approved the exact workflow.

## Required Approval

Human approval is required before Kross enters, changes, or confirms:

- bank product selection
- preparer fees
- service bureau fees
- refund transfer options
- disbursement method
- taxpayer bank details

## Kross May Do

- identify missing bank product information
- draft a task for preparer review
- compare entries against firm-approved settings
- flag inconsistency

## Kross Must Not Do

Kross must not move money, change payout settings, alter firm fees, submit bank product applications, or finalize disbursement details without approval.

## Acceptance Criteria

This SOP is working when bank product settings remain under human control.
